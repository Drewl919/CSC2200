About Me

I spent most of my life growing up in Downers Grove and attended school
out there. For high school I went to Downers Grove South and while there
I was involved in a couple different clubs, but my two favorite were
research club and radio club. In research club I worked with graduate
students from Lewis University on a research topic. The topic was studying
how to use liquid crystal to prevent lasers from striking pilots' eyes during
landing and take off. In radio club, I had my own radio show on my school's
radio station. Once I graduated from high school, I moved to North Aurora
and since this was during COVID, I continued my education at College of DuPage.
While at CoD, every class that I had was on zoom and that made my time at CoD
not very enjoyable. However, I was able to finish my associates degree in 3
semesters instead of 4. Once done, I left and transferred to Aurora University
to work towards my bachelors in cybersecurity and computer science. Some of
the things I enjoy doing include gaming on my computer and listening to music
any chance I can get. When able, I also really like being out in nature.
